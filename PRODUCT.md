# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- Customers (businesses, individuals) looking for premium bird nest packaging, glass jars, glass bottles, and related accessories in Vietnam. They need reliable, high-quality information about technical specs, pricing, and services.
- Admin staff of Đại Tài Lợi who manage the catalog, site content, recruitment, and process customer inquiries.

## Product Purpose
- Provide a premium, polished public catalog showcasing high-quality packaging products.
- Facilitate direct customer inquiries (phone, Zalo, quotation forms) to drive sales.
- Offer an intuitive, secure admin CMS to manage categories, products, inquiries, and site content.

## Brand Personality
- Professional, Premium, Trustworthy
- Traditional yet modern, clean and structured layouts, high emphasis on product quality and integrity.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing page clichés (warm cream/beige backgrounds, tiny tracked all-caps eyebrows, ghost-card borders paired with huge shadows).
- Overly cluttered, busy, or text-heavy screens with no room to breathe.
- Low-contrast text, hard-to-read fonts, or amateurish SVG/sketchy illustrations.

## Design Principles
1. **Product as Hero**: High-quality imagery, clean grids, and clear specs should make the packaging products look premium and tangible.
2. **Unshakable Trust**: Clear visual structure, readable typography (Be Vietnam Pro), and high contrast to project professionalism.
3. **Frictionless Action**: Keep the user flow from product discovery to consultation call/message as short and simple as possible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict adherence to WCAG 2.1 AA contrast standards (minimum 4.5:1 for body copy).
- Support for keyboard navigation and screen readers.
- Respect user preferences for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`).
